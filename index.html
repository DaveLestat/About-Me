All About Me

As a person who was brought up doing heavy construction work as a kid, I grew up with an affinity towards doing things the simler way. Not to be confused with doing things the cheap way, I really take pride in my work.

After serving in the Navy for six years, I really came to appreciate efficiency. Nameyly, because the Navy didn't seem to have any. I remember an old Chief whose mantra was "Work smatha, no hardda"

Truth is . . . . . I am lazy. I like to wake up, drink my milk and donuts and know that the complex project I have ahead will be much simpler because of the tools I poses. I am still driven, and take an awful lot of pride in my work. I merely don't like working too much for too little return. I have learned that if you are smart and efficient, you can accomplish the same level of output with less input. And I sure like my milk and donuts.

As a software developer, I believe I can help people achieve the same goal I have: "Efficiency through simplicity". If I can make a software or an app that does half of your work, I have already accomplished my goal. And that is why I am in CodeFellows.

Well, that, and the money. I like my milk and donuts after all.
